Revision Tips & Study Techniques

3-2-1 Revision Method

Write 3 things you learned, 2 interesting facts, and 1 question you still have. Helps consolidate learning.

Active Recall vs. Rereading

Test yourself on material instead of just rereading. Boosts memory and understanding.

SQ3R Reading Method

Survey, Question, Read, Recite, Review. Improves comprehension and retention.
